🌅 Weekly Routine Strategy

Saturday Morning Low Intensity
  • English L&L Reading: Sticky note quotes for literary devices immediately. Saves Paper 2 prep time.
  • CAS Portfolio: Update reflections weekly. Upload photos now to prevent panic later.
Sunday Morning High Priority
  • Math AA SL (07:30 - 09:00): Do this when fresh.
  • Traffic Light System: Mark questions Red/Amber/Green.
  • Mistake Notebook: Copy 'Red' questions to re-attempt blindly next week.
Sunday Evening Writing Block
  • The Writer's Gym: 19:30 - 20:30. No Reading. Only Writing.
  • Goal: One perfect PETER+L paragraph OR 300 words of "ugly" EE drafting.
Sunday Night Executive Control
  • Deadline Audit: Check ManageBac/Calendar for the next 7 days.
  • Tutor DM: Message tutors specific questions now so they can prepare.
  • Bag & Clothes: Pack gym kit & print HW tonight.

🚀 Hidden Time Pockets

🏫 School Free Periods Clear the Deck
  • Homework Rule: Finish tonight's drills at school. Ask teachers for help instantly.
  • Review Sandwich: First 5 mins: Review notes from the class you just finished.
  • Pre-Tutor: Organize questions for your evening session.
🚇 MTR Transit Strategy Adaptive
🪑 Seated (Active): Flashcards (Anki) for Bio/Chem definitions or Chinese idioms.
🧍 Standing (Passive): Listen to Econ news podcasts (RWE) or English audiobooks. Mental Rehearsal.
😴 Tired (Rest): Do not force it. Close your eyes. A rested brain learns better.

📚 Subject-Specific Tactics (Click for Notion)

🧬 HL Biology Understanding
  • Feynman: Teach concepts out loud. Stumbling = Gap.
  • IB Guide: Check syllabus statements for exact content required.
  • "Surgery": Check Mark Scheme for scoring keywords.
  • Anki: Focus on definitions.
🧪 HL Chemistry Deep Mastery
  • Recall Drills: Draw Organic pathways & VSEPR shapes from memory on blank paper. Correct in red.
  • Quant Algorithms: Write "Step-by-Step Recipes" for calculations.
  • Anki Rigor: Trends/Bonding definitions. If not word-for-word, press "Again".
📈 HL Economics Definitions
  • Def & Diagram Bank: Use Anki for verbatim definitions & drawing diagrams from memory.
  • 3-Step Drill: 1. Define. 2. Diagram (from memory). 3. Explain the shift.
  • Real World Examples: Maintain a Google Doc table with 1 news headline per week for Paper 1.
🧮 Math AASL Practice
  • Traffic Light System: Mark Qs Red/Amber/Green.
  • Mistake Notebook: Copy 'Red' questions here. Re-attempt them blindly next week.
  • Formula Booklet: Memorize locations, not just formulas.
📖 English L&L Structure
  • Skeleton Plan: Write only Topic Sentences first.
  • PETER + L Check: Point, Evidence, Technique, Effect, Reader, Link.
  • Sticky Note Habit: Mark literary devices, authorial choices & identify Global Issues.
🗣️ Chinese B Rotation
  • Paper Cycle: Rotate strictly: Reading → Listening → Writing. One per session.
  • Daily Input: Add unknown words to Anki from your daily reading/video.
  • Dictation Review: Tue (20:00) & Thu (19:00). 15 mins. Listen → Write character.
📝 Extended Essay Urgent Start
  • Thursday Sacred Hour (18:30): You must produce content here.
  • Phase 1: Bullet outline & source map.
  • Phase 2 (Ugly Draft): Write 500 words/hour. No editing.
🧠 TOK Slow Burn
  • TOK Journal: See weird news? Ask "How do we know this is true?"
  • Tag with concepts: Bias, Certainty, Truth.
